About Me

Hi, I’m Saisa — a passionate web developer, designer, and creative thinker. I love combining technology and design to create immersive experiences on the web.

My work focuses on interactive visuals, animations, and making websites feel alive. I believe the web isn’t just about text and images — it’s about crafting an atmosphere.

Beyond coding, I’m curious about space, physics, and the mysteries of the universe — which inspires much of my design style.
